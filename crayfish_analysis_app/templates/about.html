Our Mission

The population of the invasive signal crayfish is increasing rapidly which is harming the ecosystem and biodiversity as they consume the surrounding aquatic life. They also damage public infrastructure by burrowing into the riverbanks. Current crayfish trapping methods are not very effective. Therefore, We aim to provide scientists with a reliable tool that can help them locate and minor the signal crayfish population.

Our Team

Our team consists of four undergraduate university students from different engineering disciplines. We are a group of passionate individuals who are serious about the environment and are committed to be working on this project. Due to our different academic backgrounds, we are able to utilise a range of different skills and knowledge to help us achieve our goal.
